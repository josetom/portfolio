Pricing has evolved from a set-it-and-forget-it financial decision to a dynamic GTM lever that companies pull continuously.

In the AI era, products evolve fast and customer value perception shifts constantly. But as usage patterns become more complex, many teams paradoxically become slower at iterating pricing.

After studying AI and SaaS companies struggling with usage-based billing, one pattern stood out: the problem is often architectural. Systems built to enable flexibility can end up creating rigidity.

That’s why pricing agility is a core design objective in Chargebee’s Usage-Based Billing approach—from schema-flexible ingestion to modular billing layers that let teams iterate without heavy engineering bottlenecks.
